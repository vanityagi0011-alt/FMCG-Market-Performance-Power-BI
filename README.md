📌 Project Overview
This project analyzes global Fast-Moving Consumer Goods (FMCG) sales data sourced from Kaggle. Using Power BI, I built interactive dashboards to uncover revenue patterns, profit drivers, and regional performance across 7 global regions and 12 product categories.The goal was to identify which product categories and regions generate the most revenue and profit — insights that are critical for business decision-making in the FMCG industry.

🗂️ Dataset Details
PropertyDetailsSourceKaggle — Global Sales DatasetRecords100 transactionsTime Period2010 – 2017Regions7 (Asia, Europe, North America, etc.)

🔍 Key Insights
💄 Cosmetics emerged as the highest revenue-generating category (₹36.60M), driven by the largest gap between unit price and unit cost — highest gross margin per unit
🗂️ Office Supplies ranked 2nd in revenue (₹30.59M) with 44,727 total units sold
🏠 Household ranked 3rd in revenue (₹29.89M) with 31,972 total units sold
🌎 Central America & the Caribbean recorded the highest total revenue by region (6.09M), making it the top-performing market
